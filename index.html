Émile Jaques-Dalcroze

Music and movement teacher

Some important dates from Dalcroze's life:

  • 1865 - Born in Vienna
  • 1877 - Joined the Conservatoire de Musique, where he studied with Anton Bruckner
  • 1892 - He became a professor of harmony at the Geneva Conservatory
  • 1910 - He established his own school in Hellerau, near Dresden
  • 1914 - Dalcroze came back to Geneva to open a new institute
  • 1950 - Dalcroze died in Geneva

"The Dalcroze method, also known as Dalcroze Eurhythmics, is another approach music educators use to foster music appreciation, ear-training, and improvisation while improving musical abilities. In this method, the body is the main instrument."
